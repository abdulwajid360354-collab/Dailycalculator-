Privacy & Data Policy.

Transparency is the foundation of trust. Learn how we maintain a zero-storage calculation environment to protect your sensitive information.

1. Commitment to Absolute Anonymity

At DailyCalculator, we operate on a fundamentally different framework than traditional utility websites. We understand that our users entrust us with sensitive financial, health, and engineering data. Our platform is engineered as a Zero-Database Ecosystem. This means that we do not store, log, or maintain any of the input values you enter into our 210+ precision tools.

All calculations are processed in real-time within your local browser environment. Once you close your tab or refresh the page, your data vanishes permanently. This "Ephemeral Processing" model ensures that your fiscal and biometric information remains entirely under your control.

2. Information We Never Collect

  • Financial inputs (Loan amounts, Salaries, Tax figures).
  • Biometric data (Weight, Height, Age, Health history).
  • Engineering dimensions or project specifications.
  • Personal Identifiable Information (Names, Emails, Phone numbers).

3. Log Files and Analytical Data

Like any successful global platform, DailyCalculator follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.

The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users' movement on the website, and gathering demographic information. None of this data is linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

4. Advertising Partners (Adsterra)

To keep our 210+ professional tools 100% free for the global community, we partner with third-party advertising vendors like Adsterra. These vendors use cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons that are used in their respective advertisements and links that appear on DailyCalculator.

These third-party ad servers automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. They use these technologies to measure the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see on websites that you visit. Please note that DailyCalculator has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

5. PDF Download Security

Our professional PDF Report Generation feature creates documents on the client-side. When you click "Download Audit," the PDF is generated by your browser's processing power. At no point is the generated report uploaded to our servers. Your professional audits remain private and local.

6. Contacting the Laboratory

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact our technical team at support@dailycalculator.com.
