5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does binary subtraction work?

Binary subtraction often utilizes the "borrow" method similar to decimal math. In professional environments, computers use Two's Complement to perform subtraction via addition logic.

What is a 'Bit'?

A bit is the smallest unit of data in computing, representing a binary digit. It can exist in one of two states: 0 (Off/False) or 1 (On/True).
