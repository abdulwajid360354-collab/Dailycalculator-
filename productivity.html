2. The Mathematical Formula for Productivity Audit

Our laboratory utilizes the verified algebraic frameworks used by global consulting firms and the Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) to ensure 100% parity with international standards:

Productivity Ratio = Total Output / Total Input (Hours or Dollars)
  • Total Output: The total revenue realized or quantity of goods produced.
  • Labor Input: The summation of all work hours committed to the production cycle.
  • Multifactor Productivity: An advanced state where capital, labor, and materials are audited simultaneously.
  • Marginal Return: The additional output generated by adding one unit of labor.
